Chef John's Yucatan-Style Grilled Pork

Description

This is very quick and easy pasta salad. Best for picnics and BBQ's.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce package tri-colored pasta assortment
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 large cucumber, peeled and diced
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 16 ounce bottle Italian-style salad dressing

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in lightly salted water until al dente. Drain and rinse in cold water.
  2. In large bowl combine pasta with tomatoes, onion, cucumbers and Italian dressing. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
